About Smithing

Smithing is the art of forging metal into weapons, armor, and tools. Adventurers rely on skilled blacksmiths for everything from banded armor to ornate cultural gear.

Key Components

  • Smithy Hammer
  • Forge
  • Ore (Small/Large Brick of Ore)
  • Water Flask

Common Recipes

  • Banded Mail: Sheet Metal + Leather Padding
  • Metal Bits: Small Pieces of Ore + Water
  • Ornate Weapons: Cultural Templates + Enchanted Bars

Skill-Up Tips

Start with metal bits and banded armor, then graduate to cultural smithing. Always use a geerlok to improve success rates!

May your forge burn bright and your fails be few.
