About Tinkering

Tinkering is the exclusive domain of Gnomes — a mechanical madness of gadgets, gizmos, and glorious explosions. Whether it’s summoning a bank or making your own hover device, tinkerers are the tech wizards of Norrath.

Materials & Tools

  • Tinkering Tools (Portable Toolbox)
  • Gears, Cogs, Grease, Power Supplies
  • Metal Bits, Clockwork Grease, Mana Batteries

Useful Inventions

  • Geerlok Devices: Tradeskill buff items
  • Mechanical Net Launcher: Pulling tool
  • Deluxe Toolbox: Bigger bag, essential for adventurers
  • Rebreathers, Shrink Devices, Banker Bots: Practical magic meets metal

Skill-Up Tips

Start with basic gears and combine recipes sold by Tinkering vendors in Ak’Anon. Level up using batteries and Geerlok plans. It’s not cheap — but it’s explosive fun!

If it sparks, clanks, or hisses — you're doing it right.
