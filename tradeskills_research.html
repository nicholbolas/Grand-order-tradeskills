About Research

Research is the scholarly skill of creating spell scrolls using runes, words, and powders. It’s used mostly by INT casters — Wizards, Enchanters, Necromancers, Magicians — to make spells otherwise hard to find.

Tools & Ingredients

  • Research Container (Lexicon or Tome)
  • Words, Runes, Pages (mob drops)
  • Spell Research Kits (for newer combines)
  • Binding Powders, Ink Additives, Quills

Common Research Projects

  • Level 51+ Spells: Often researched instead of bought
  • Ancient Spells: Rare and powerful versions
  • Class-Specific Combines: Recipes vary by caster type

Skill-Up Tips

Old-school research requires hunting for pages and combining them. Modern research often uses vendor-sold components. Find a vendor loop, stock up on powder and parchment, and let the study session begin.

May your mind stay sharp and your parchments stay dry.
