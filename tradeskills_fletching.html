About Fletching

Fletching is the art of crafting arrows, bows, and ranged weapon components. Rangers love it, vendors tolerate it, and Norrathian wildlife fears it.

Key Materials

  • Wood Shafts & Arrowheads
  • Fletching Tools (Fletching Kit)
  • Feathers (e.g. Griffon, Owlbear)
  • Silken Strings, Bow Staves

Popular Recipes

  • Class 5 Steel Point Arrows: Mid-to-high level damage arrows
  • Trivial Bows: Skill up with basic staves + silk string
  • Tailored Fletching: Combine with tailoring for cultural bows

Skill-Up Tips

Start with simple arrows (field point heads + shafts + fletch). Move up to bows as you gain skill. Vendor materials make the early grind easy and affordable.

May your shots fly true and your combines never miss.
