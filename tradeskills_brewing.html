About Brewing

Brewing isn’t just about booze — it’s about buffs, poisons, and stamina restoration. Whether you're supplying ogres with ale or making stat drinks for raids, brewing has your back.

Common Ingredients

  • Water Flasks & Malt
  • Hops, Barley, Yeast
  • Mead, Spirits, Berries
  • Brewer's Barrel

Popular Recipes

  • Kaladim Constitutional: A high-CHA drink made from rare ingredients
  • Fish Wine: Tacky, cheap, and good for skillups
  • Heady Kiola: A vital ingredient in many cultural tailoring combines

Skill-Up Tips

Start with bog juice and fish wine — cheap, stackable, and easy. Once higher, try Heady Kiola and Constitutionals for profit and practicality.

May your brews never sour and your tank never fall.
