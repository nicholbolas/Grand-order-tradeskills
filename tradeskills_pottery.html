About Pottery

Pottery is a patient art, turning clay and elemental magic into urns, idols, spell components, and more. While not the flashiest skill, it's essential to spellcasters and cultural crafters.

Materials & Tools

  • Unfired Clay, Water Flask, Sculpting Tools
  • Pottery Wheel (combine) & Kiln (firing)
  • Enchanted Clay, Gem Encrusting Tools

Popular Creations

  • Small/Large Urns: Used in cultural recipes and quests
  • Idols & Deity Symbols: Key to cultural armor combines
  • Spell Research Components: Crucial for casters

Skill-Up Tips

Start with unfired bowls or mugs — easy to make and cheap. Fire them in a kiln to raise skill. Later, focus on deity idols or research clay for profit and usefulness.

Spin it, fire it, enchant it — and try not to drop it.
