About Poison Making

Poison Making is a rogue-only skill used to craft deadly concoctions that can be applied to weapons for devastating effects. It’s tactical, it’s dangerous, and it smells *terrible*.

Tools & Ingredients

  • Mortar and Pestle (Poisoncraft Kit)
  • Suspensions, Extracts, Venoms, Toxins
  • Rare components from mobs or vendors
  • Poison Vials (crafted or purchased)

Popular Poisons

  • Sting of the Scorpion: Damage-over-time poison
  • Elixir of Disorientation: Brief stun effect
  • Nerve Burn: Slows enemy movement or attack speed

Skill-Up Tips

Start with basic suspensions and low-tier poisons. Combine vendor-sold components and slowly mix in foraged/dropped rare ingredients. Keep your rogue stealthed and deadly at all times.
