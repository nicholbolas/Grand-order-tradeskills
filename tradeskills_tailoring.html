About Tailoring

Tailoring is the art of crafting armor, bags, and robes from hides, silks, and various woven materials. It’s essential for leather-wearers, monks, and stylish casters alike.

Key Materials

  • Silk Swatches
  • Animal Pelts (Bear, Cat, Spider)
  • Studs, Boning, and Patterns
  • Loom

Common Recipes

  • Handmade Backpack: 1 High Quality Bear Pelt + Boning + Pattern
  • Wu's Fighting Armor: Silk Swatches + Curing Agents + Patterns
  • Cultural Robes: Rare Silks + Enchanted Components

Skill-Up Tips

Start with patchwork or raw silk, then work up to reinforced or Wu’s sets. Consider using a sewing kit for combines, and don’t forget your geerlok tailoring device for best results.

May your seams hold tight and your rolls crit high.
