About Jewelcraft

Jewelcraft transforms raw gemstones and metals into enchanted rings, earrings, and symbols of power. It's a skill of precision, magic, and a fair bit of platinum.

Materials & Tools

  • Cut Gems (sold or foraged)
  • Metal Bars: Gold, Silver, Electrum, Platinum, Velium
  • Jeweler's Kit
  • Enchanting Spells (for enchanted metal bars)

Popular Creations

  • Platinum Fire Wedding Ring: Affordable, great CHA
  • Velium Diamond Ring: High-end INT/WIS for casters
  • Enchanted Earrings: Useful for quests and twinks alike
